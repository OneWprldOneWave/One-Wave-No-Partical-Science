Here's the cleaned-up summary of the Branches to Bricks: Yellow Brick Road app as we've evolved it.


---

Branches to Bricks: Yellow Brick Road

Core Purpose

A knowledge-building, validation, and collaboration ecosystem.

The goal is not simply to collect ideas.

The goal is to turn ideas into foundations, foundations into roads, roads into bridges, and bridges into new opportunities.


---

Forest

The forest represents all unexplored knowledge.

Forest
=
Unknown Space

Everything not yet understood.


---

Trees

Trees represent subjects or domains.

Examples:

Physics
Biology
Education
Music
AI
Engineering
Stories

Each tree grows its own ideas.


---

Roots

Roots represent hidden connections.

Roots
=
Relationships

Roots may connect distant trees.

AI can discover root connections humans miss.

A root connection may create entirely new ideas.


---

Apples

Apples are ideas.

Apple
=
Idea

Most apples are ordinary.

Some apples are golden apples.


---

Golden Apples

Golden apples are unusually promising ideas.

Golden Apple
=
High-Value Clue

Not proven.

Not validated.

Simply important enough to investigate first.


---

Branches

Branches are hypotheses.

Branch
=
Possibility

A branch is a direction worth exploring.

Branches can grow from:

Apples

Golden Apples

Root connections

Existing roads

AI discoveries



---

---

Yellow Brick

Yellow Brick
=
Under Evaluation

A chosen path.

The beginning of a road.


---

Green Brick

Green Brick
=
Connected Foundation

The brick has survived initial testing.

The brick is connected to root systems.

The brick contributes to the road.


---

Lead Brick

Lead Brick
=
Strong Internal Foundation

Heavy.

Useful.

Supports weight.

Internally validated.

Ready for external testing.


---

Bronze Brick

Bronze Brick
=
Early External Support

Some outside evidence exists.

More testing required.


---

Silver Brick

Silver Brick
=
Strong External Support

Repeated success.

Multiple validations.


---

Gold Brick

Gold Brick
=
Reliable Knowledge

Survived extensive contact with reality.

Still revisable.

But trusted.


---

Roads

Roads connect validated bricks.

Road
=
Connected Foundations

Roads provide direction.

Roads help people move through knowledge.


---

Mature Trees

A mature tree has produced most of its apples.

Most important branches have been explored.

Most useful ideas have become bricks.


---

Wood

Wood comes from mature trees.

Wood
=
Communication
Documentation
Teaching
Standards

Wood is not knowledge.

Wood is how knowledge is shared.


---

Bridges

Bridges connect roads to people.

Bridge
=
Collaboration

Bridges may connect to:

Schools

Universities

Nonprofits

Open-source projects

Research groups

Maker spaces

Communities

Organizations may require:

Specific Trees

before investing resources.

Not requested.

Launch Pad

---

Rockets

---

---

Core Philosophy

Roads solve problems.

Bridges share solutions.

Launch Pads create new frontiers.

Rockets reach new mysteries.

And the final rule:

The goal is not to run out of problems.

The goal is to have better problems.
