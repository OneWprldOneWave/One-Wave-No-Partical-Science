Statement:

Local structures may possess boundaries.

The connectivity lattice relating those structures may not possess the same boundaries.

Objects may be bounded.

Relationships may not be bounded.

Current Interpretation:

3D Expressed Space

- Excitations possess boundaries.
- Quarks possess boundaries.
- Protons possess boundaries.
- Local closures possess boundaries.

2D Compressed Space

- The lattice is the connectivity map.
- Nodes represent local structures.
- Edges represent coupling relationships.
- The lattice may extend beyond any individual bounded structure.

Question:

Are boundaries properties of local closures while the underlying lattice extends across scales?
